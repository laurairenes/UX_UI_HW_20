Petal

People struggle to manage their time and complete tasks due to stress, distractions, and overwhelm. They need support building healthy daily habits, organizing their daily tasks, and seeing their tasks to completion.

Petal gives users a place to organize, set goals & reminders, and learn habits to stay on track in all aspects of their lives.

Conducted Five 1-on-1 interviews, had 10 survey respondents, 10 user tests

Pain points included finishing tasks, feeling overwhelmed, prioritizing tasks, receiving quality information about mental health

Interviewees were motivated to be organized and complete tasks, but were often overwhlemed and strugled to maintain a consistent routine

PROBLEM STATEMENT

People struggle to manage their time and complete tasks due to stress, distractions, and overwhelm. They need support building healthy daily habits, organizing their daily tasks, and seeing their tasks to completion.

ITERATIONS

During testing we found that cards were too transparent and hard to read. We had to play with background blurring and opacity to make things readable while staying within our soft and soothing design.

Features were clunky when adding tags for “Add New Goal” feature. Field obscured by keyboard. The User Quiz check boxes too small, increase size to avoid missing intended field. The “Journal updated” overlay is too transparent, not readable, so we increased opacity.
